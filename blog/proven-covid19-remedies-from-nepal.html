Natural COVID-19 Remedies from Nepal: Boosting Immunity the Traditional Way

Published on July 28, 2025 in Nutrition & Diet

As the COVID-19 pandemic continues to challenge the world, strengthening immunity has become more important than ever. In Nepal, people have turned to time-tested Ayurvedic herbs and natural remedies to protect themselves against the virus 🪔. While modern medicine plays a crucial role, the Nepalese approach highlights the power of organic, traditional methods in building resilience and managing symptoms. The results have been inspiring, showing how nature can support our health in times of crisis.

Nepal’s Remarkable Response to COVID-19

In 2020, experts predicted a devastating COVID-19 outbreak in Nepal, estimating nearly 850,000 cases and tens of thousands of deaths. However, the reality was far less severe. By September 2020, Nepal reported only 45,236 cases and 300 deaths - a stark contrast to the predictions. This success is largely credited to the use of traditional remedies and a deep-rooted cultural practice of relying on natural immunity boosters.

The Healing Power of Ayurvedic Herbs

For generations, Nepalese communities - especially the Gorkhali people - have trusted Ayurvedic herbs and organic practices to stay healthy 🌿. Natural remedies are packed with anti-inflammatory, anti-viral, and immune-boosting properties. Here are 11 powerful herbs and fruits commonly used in Nepal:

  • Gurjo (Tinospora Sinensis): Known for its immune-boosting and anti-inflammatory benefits.
  • Coriander Seeds (Dhania): Rich in antioxidants that detoxify the body and strengthen immunity.
  • Aasuro (Justicia Adhatoda): A go-to for respiratory health with anti-viral properties.
  • Ginger (Aaduwa): Acts as a natural anti-inflammatory and soothes the body 🍵.
  • Thyme Seeds (Juwano): Treat coughs and respiratory issues due to anti-microbial properties.
  • Garlic (Lasun): A powerful immune booster with anti-viral and anti-bacterial benefits.
  • Turmeric (Besar): Contains curcumin - a natural anti-inflammatory and immunity enhancer.
  • Holy Basil (Tulsi): Fights infections and reduces stress while strengthening immunity.
  • Aamla (Indian Gooseberry): Packed with Vitamin C and a strong antioxidant 💪.
  • Cinnamon (Dalchini): Known for anti-viral properties and often mixed with honey or water.
  • Lemon (Kagati): A rich source of Vitamin C, helps detoxify the body.

How Nepalese People Use These Remedies

  • Herbal Teas and Juices: Herbs and fruits are boiled to create soothing teas or juices.
  • Inhalation: Steam from boiled herbs is used to clear nasal passages and support breathing.
  • Direct Consumption: Ingredients like garlic and ginger are consumed raw or added to meals.

Why These Remedies Are Effective

  • They strengthen immunity and help the body fight off infections.
  • They reduce inflammation caused by COVID-19.
  • They detoxify the body and promote well-being.
  • They support respiratory health and ease breathing problems.

A Friendly Reminder

While these remedies have helped many, they are not a replacement for medical treatment. If you suspect you have COVID-19 or develop serious symptoms, consult a healthcare professional right away ⚠️. These natural solutions can complement medical care - but not replace it.

By learning from Nepal’s traditional practices, you can explore safe, natural ways to boost your immunity. Whether it’s sipping ginger tea, adding turmeric to your meals, or enjoying a warm glass of lemon water, small steps can have a big impact. Embrace the wisdom of nature and take charge of your health today 🌼.
