Calculate Your Body Mass Index

BMI is a simple number that helps estimate whether your weight is in a healthy range for your height. It is best used as a general screening tool, not a medical diagnosis.

BMI Categories

Category BMI Range Meaning
Underweight Below 18.5 May indicate you need more calories or nutrients.
Normal weight 18.5 to 24.9 Generally considered a healthy BMI range.
Overweight 25 to 29.9 May suggest excess body weight for height.
Obese 30 and above May increase the risk of certain health conditions.

What BMI Can Tell You

BMI gives a quick estimate of body weight relative to height. It is useful for a general overview, but it does not measure muscle mass, bone density, or body fat distribution.

When BMI is Less Useful

BMI may be less accurate for athletes, pregnant people, and anyone with unusually high or low muscle mass. For a full health picture, talk with a healthcare professional.
